# Point Brand Voice Examples

## Hero Copy

Before:

```text
Get cash now from your house with our amazing home equity products.
```

After:

```text
Unlock your home equity for what matters next.
Compare personalized HEI and HELOC options with no risk to your credit score.
```

Why it works:

- Leads with the homeowner outcome.
- Names the product choice.
- Uses a low-pressure CTA context.
- Avoids hype.

## HEI Explanation

Before:

```text
Our HEI gets you money with no payments and you can use it however you want.
```

After:

```text
A Home Equity Investment gives you upfront funds in exchange for a share of your home's future value. There are no monthly payments, and you choose when to buy back your equity during the 30-year term.
```

Why it works:

- Makes the exchange explicit.
- Preserves the no-monthly-payment benefit.
- Explains control and term length without overpromising.

## HELOC Explanation

Before:

```text
A HELOC is the fastest way to use your home's cash.
```

After:

```text
A HELOC is a line of credit secured by your home. For qualified homeowners, it can provide fast access to funds and monthly payments based on what you draw.
```

Why it works:

- Defines the product plainly.
- Qualifies speed and eligibility.
- Clarifies payment structure.

## Comparison Copy

Before:

```text
HEI is better than a HELOC because there are no monthly payments.
```

After:

```text
An HEI may fit if you want upfront funds without adding a monthly payment. A HELOC may fit if you qualify for a line of credit and want to draw funds as needed.
```

Why it works:

- Helps the homeowner choose based on need.
- Avoids an unsupported universal ranking.
- Accurately separates product mechanics.

## CTA Options

Use:

- "View my offers"
- "See what I qualify for"
- "Compare my options"
- "Check my eligibility"
- "Get my cost estimate"
- "Learn how the HEI works"
- "Learn how the HELOC works"

Avoid:

- "Claim my cash"
- "Get approved now"
- "Solve my debt today"
- "Unlock free money"
- "Take the deal"
